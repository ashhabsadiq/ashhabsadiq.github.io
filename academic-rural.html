A SMART Village Proposal for Sadakpur Village, Cumilla, Bangladesh

Objective

The objective of this study is to assess existing conditions and develop smart interventions for sustainable rural development in Sadakpur Village, Cumilla.

Study Area

Sadakpur village is located in Pirjatrapur Union under Burichang Upazila of Cumilla district. It is a small rural settlement where most residents depend on agriculture and dairy farming for their livelihood, with some involvement in poultry-based micro-enterprises. Despite its economic potential, the village faces limited infrastructure and service access, which affects overall living conditions. Its location and existing livelihood structure make it a suitable case for exploring smart, integrated rural development approaches.

Summary

This project proposes a Smart Village Plan for Sadakpur to enhance rural productivity, accessibility, and livelihood opportunities through technology-driven interventions. The plan focuses on smart agriculture, hybrid solar irrigation, rural transport improvement, and micro-enterprise development (poultry sector).
